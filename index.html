Hi, my name is Sara Johansson. I have worked as a journalist since 2005. the dream of living on writing and being a journalist came when I was 15 years old. I started in the social sciences program with a focus on media. I then applied up the country to Sundsvall and the Central University where I studied media and communication science, which I took a file, bachelor's degree in. I also studied graphic design and newspaper design to broaden my knowledge. Then I studied journalism at Kaggeholms university and got the opportunity for several internships, including at Eskilstuna Courier, Folkbladet and Expressen.
